Meet ZIGAM

We're building a modern, dignified ecosystem for homemaking — for homes and for the youth who power them.

Our Story

ZIGAM was created to bridge a crucial gap: the rising demand for trusted domestic help — and the untapped potential of Nigeria's vibrant youth looking for flexible, respectable work.

We modernize homemaking by offering homes peace of mind and offering workers dignity, training, and purpose.

What We Stand For

Vision

To bridge the gap between the growing need for reliable support in households and the vast potential of youths and young adults, by creating meaningful employment opportunities that empower them to serve with purpose and dignity.

Mission

To transform the domestic services sector, creating a modern, safe, and reliable ecosystem that ensures fair opportunities for employees and dependable solutions for employers.

Values

Integrity • Proactivity • Empathy • Diligence
