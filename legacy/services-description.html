Description of Services

"At Zigam, we create more than clean and organised spaces — we create time, comfort, and peace of mind, allowing our clients to focus on living and performing at their best."

Below are our services and what a customer should expect, especially for our One-time services and our Ozi Subscription service.

Errand Concierge Service

Let Zigam handle the tasks that keep life moving. Our Errands Service helps busy individuals, families, businesses and professionals save time by outsourcing routine tasks and daily responsibilities.

Ideal for: Busy professionals, families, elderly clients, and anyone seeking more time for what matters most.

Food Processing & Kitchen Assistance

Enjoy the convenience of a well-prepared kitchen without the hassle. We handle the time-consuming groundwork so you can focus on the final cooking or the business at hand, strictly adhering to our "Zigam Clean & Safe" hygiene protocols.

Ideal for: Busy households, businesses, working professionals, large families, and clients preparing for events or special occasions.

Laundry

Thoughtful wardrobe and linen care, delivered with attention to detail.

Decluttering & Organising

Transform your space into a more functional, beautiful, and stress-free environment. Our service helps create order, maximise space, and improve the efficiency of your home or workspace.

Ideal for: Individuals and businesses seeking a more organised lifestyle, families, professionals, and anyone looking to create a calmer, more productive living environment.

General Standard Cleaning & Office Cleaning

Everyday cleaning for homes and offices — sweeping, mopping, dusting, and surface sanitisation to keep your space fresh and welcoming.

Airbnb / Short-let Cleaning

Turnover-ready cleaning to keep your short-let space guest-ready.

Move-in Cleaning

A thorough cleaning of your new home in preparation for you to move in. Homes significantly larger than others of the same size, or bookings running longer than estimated, may attract additional charges, which will be communicated by our Booking Support Team.

Deep Cleaning

A more thorough approach than standard general cleaning. It includes moving furniture for better access, interior window cleaning, shelf cleaning, inside-of-kitchen-cabinet cleaning, fridge/freezer cleaning, oven cleaning, balcony cleaning, and fan cleaning — in addition to a more in-depth delivery of the standard cleaning tasks. Additional charges for unusually large homes or longer bookings will be communicated by our Relationship Manager.

Post-Construction Cleaning

Dust, debris, and detailing for newly built or renovated spaces.

Couch & Rug Cleaning

Sofa, cushion, upholstery and rug detailing.

Elderly Care & Companionship

Compassionate light caregiving, companionship, and daily support.

Childcare

Attentive childminding support. Please note: one parent or guardian must remain on the premises for the entire duration of the booking.

What We Expect

Our services can only be carried out if you have running or adequately stored water within the premises.

  • Toilet cleaner
  • Cleaning chemicals
  • Dish soap & sponges
  • Scouring powder
  • Stain remover
  • Glass cleaner
  • Cobweb broom
  • Duster
  • Air freshener
  • Laundry detergent & conditioner

Please note that for some of our one-off services, Zigam will provide the materials needed. This will be communicated in writing before confirmation of such service.
